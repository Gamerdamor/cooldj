Music Commands

Clear

Clears the current queue.

Filter

Show the filters commands.

Jump

Jump to a specific track in the queue.

Loop

Change the loop mode (autoplay|track|queue|off).

Lyrics

Get lyrics for a song.

Nowplaying

Shows details of the currently playing song (type 1 in index).

Pause

Pauses the current playing song.

Play

Play a song or playlist from url or name.

Queue

Shows the queue.

Remove

Removes a specific song from the queue.

Resume

Resumes the current paused song.

Seek

Seeks to a specific position in the current song.

Shuffle

Shuffles the queue.

Skip

Skips the current song.

Stop

Stops the playback.

Volume

Check or change the volume.
